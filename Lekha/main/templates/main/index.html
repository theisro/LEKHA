LEKHA supports the archival needs of all kinds of arts & culture practitioners.
Be it videos, images, sound or text, LEKHA allows you to upload files across a range of file formats. A familiar file-browser interface also makes it painless to organise your files and folders.

Files in archives often require some basic metadata. LEKHA lets you add that information quickly and accurately using globally-recognized standards. Choose from a range of presets, or create your own custom tags and descriptors.

Why use LEKHA?
Find out how it's built to suit your needs →

LEKHA lets you design and build archives. It allows you to add comprehensive metadata to all your files. It also allows you to exhibit your archives and find a broader audience for your collections. If you chose to keep your files and archives private, it allows you to do that in a secure manner. In short, it lets anyone not just professional archivist make archives.

No. LEKHA was built by artists with a concern for making archives easy to use. You don't have to be a rocket scientist to build an archive anymore. However, even rocket scientists are free to build archives on LEKHA. LEKHA is for anyone who wants a digital archive. This could be institutions, collectives, collectors, museums.

LEKHA is currently free while we run our beta tests. If you would like to contribute to the development of LEKHA, please get in touch with us through the contact page.

You own the files. The entity that uploads files on LEKHA owns the files. LEKHA does not own any of the files that are archived.

How will you use LEKHA?
Lekha is built to suit the needs of individuals as well as institutions
As an Individual or a Collective
Create an archive with tags in no time. Keep it private or share your archives with the world.
As an Institution
Add metadata with little effort. Translate between standards or make your own standard. Keep files in safe, secure archives. Share your archives and allow everyone to go through your archives in a fun, educational manner.
