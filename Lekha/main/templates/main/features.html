What can LEKHA help you do?
LEKHA lets you securely upload, organise and catalog your files online. Add metadata to your files, exhibit them, share them or keep it private. Lekha lets you build archives effortlessly and quickly.
See Features ↓


Archive any File
We don’t play favourites. Be it image, video, sound, text or even virtual reality, archive any file format. If you chose to exhibit your archive, visitors can view these files on any browser. LEKHA's design turns navigating your archives into a pleasurable experience.




Metadata made easy
Files in archives often require metadata. This is often a frustrating and time consuming process. LEKHA lets you add that information quickly and accurately using globally recognized archival metadata standards. Choose from a range of tags, or create your own custom tags and descriptors. Convert from one standard to another. Export your metadata. LEKHA lets search engines find your public archives and lets you find items in your private archives. LEKHA even allows you to create your own standards. LEKHA will soon offer machine learning algorithms to make the task of adding metadata less cumbersome and more accurate.




Exhibit your work
Whether you want to make a public archive to share your work, or a private archive to manage and your own files, LEKHA makes it easy to have all your work online in one place. Every archive can have a private and public component to it. If you chose to make your archive public, LEKHA's design will help you let visitors to your archive have a flawless experience navigating through your items. We believe that searching and navigating archives should be a fun, educational and pleasurable experience. LEKHA works with eminent and renowned curators to organise exhibitions of public archives.




Secure archives
Worried about the safety of your files and your archives? LEKHA has you covered. We offer military-grade AES-256 standard encryption, so that you can securely store and share your files on the web.




Copyright or Copyleft?
Chose between a range of creative commons licenses or open source licenses for your items or choose to copyright them. The choice is upto you.
